Many calculations are based on the millisecond, knowing full well you do not know your age to the millisecond. The reason for that is to give the page more dynamism.

The following constants are used in my calculations:

  • Planck times in a millisecond: @AgeCalculator.planckTimesInMillisecond
  • Age of the Earth in years: @AgeCalculator.earhAgeInYears
  • Age of the Universe in years: @AgeCalculator.universeAgeInYears
  • Voyager 1 speed in km/h (in calculations assumed to be constant thoughout the journey): @AgeCalculator.voyagerOneSpeedKmh
  • Distance in km to Proxima Centauri: @AgeCalculator.distanceToProximaCentauriKm
  • Neptune, distance from Earth in km: @AgeCalculator.neptuneFromEarth

Speed in terms of Speed of Light (c) and the associated Lorentz Terms (γ):

  • Voyager 1: @AgeCalculator.speeds("voyager")c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("voyager")
  • 90% the speed of light: @AgeCalculator.speeds("90c")c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("90c")
  • 99% the speed of light: @AgeCalculator.speeds("99c")c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("99c")
  • Large Hadron Collider (LHC) speed: @AgeCalculator.speeds("LHC")c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("LHC")
  • Large Electron-Positron Collider (LEP) speed: @AgeCalculator.speeds("LEP")c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("LEP")
  • The Oh-My-God Particle speed: 0.9999999999999999999999951c, γ=@AgeCalculator.lorentzTerm("OMG")

Body:

  • Olympic swimming pool size in litres: @AgeCalculator.olympicSwimmingPoolSizeLitres
  • Breaths per second: @AgeCalculator.breathsPerSecond
  • Heartbeats per millisecond: @AgeCalculator.heartbeatsPerMillisecond
  • Kilocalories (kcal) per second: @AgeCalculator.kilocaloriesPerSecond
